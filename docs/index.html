About

Nestled in the heart of Toronto, Canada, Trajectory is more than just a workspaceâ€”it’s a hub for collaboration in the pursuit of a safe and beneficial AI future. Designed specifically for professionals and researchers dedicated to AI safety, Trajectory provides a focused environment where rigorous analysis and innovative solutions are cultivated to reduce the most serious risks from AI’s rapid development.

At Trajectory, we understand the critical importance of safeguarding against potential risks associated with AI development. Our focus is on identifying, analyzing, and mitigating these risks to ensure that AI systems remain aligned with human values and interests.

Trajectory is located at 100 University Ave, and is free to use for approved members working on reducing catastrophic AI risk. You can apply to become a member using this form.

Trajectory is funded by Open Philanthropy through Effective Altruism Canada.
